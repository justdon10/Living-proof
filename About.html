About Living Proof



Living Proof church is a place where we come together to worship, Praise, serve the Lord . The church is under the Reedem Christian church of God (RCCG) ...RCCG was founded by Rev. Josiah Akindayomi and now overseen by Pastor E.A Adeboye . Living Proof church has grown over the years to be a great house of God
